Crusty Krab Crew Callsigns
Descriptions of callsigns as seen in Personnel. All callsigns are generated with a number of digits equivalent to the unit's rank index plus one. Pending units and resigned units carry 6-digit callsigns regardless of rank index.
It is proper to refer to a unit by their full callsign including the letter, codenames or legal names in official venues, and when in time-constricted scenarios any unique identifier (always subject to rebuttal via popular vote) is acceptable - nicknames being an excellent example.
Military Callsigns
These start with M.
Non-Military Callsigns
These start with C for 'civil' or 'clerical' or with Mg for management.
Special Callsigns & Designations
[Other Callsign Type]-P : Pending unit.
[Other Callsign Type]-Rs : Resigned unit.
Departmental/Senority Annexations
A departmental or senority-designating annexation can be concatonated to the end of a callsign to designate departments. Multiple of these may be designated at any time.
C123456-IT : Intelligence technologies (database) civil district
M123456-R : Reserve corps of CKCMC
X123456-A : Administrative subtype of any division
X123456-LA : Lower management subtype of any division as appointed by division admin
