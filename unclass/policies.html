Crusty Krab Crew Policies
1. Division heads manage their division in any way they see fit without supervision (one exception noted below) unless rebutted by a full vote. This includes but is not limited to ranking systems, assignments, and awarding (with supervision from upper management).
2. Crew member induction is, from 10/25/24 on, to be conducted by the jurisdiction of the highest-ranking official of a division (or the person to whom this official has entrusted this responsibility), where this official can only induct members to their own division. These inductions should not be announced prior to induction to avoid corruption through alteration of policy.
3. Any crew member reserves the right to show themselves off-duty.
