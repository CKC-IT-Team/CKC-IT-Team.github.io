Ranking Information
The IT division operates on a merit-based hierarchical system with an elected administrator (as with any other division). Rankings and their assignments are defined directly by said administrator.
Rankings, in order of senority, are as follows:
Lead Technician
Lower administrative position in the department. Not restriced to one unit. Typically assumes CI/CD Admin role on the repository. As yet the most senior position excepting division administrator.
Senior Technician
As yet highest non-administrative position in the department. Typically assumes maintain role on the repository. Permitted to use lower technicians as direct subordinates, bar an order from administrator or lead tech.
Technician
Typically assumes write role on the repository. Not permitted to use lower technicians as direct subordinates bar becoming their senior technician's trustee.
Junior Technician
As yet lowest position in the department. Typically assumes triage role on the repository.
